Executive Overview
What the product is, why it mattered, and what I was responsible for

This product started as a failing LMS in a large acute NHS Trust. It had to support learning compliance, digital appraisals, and workforce processes in an environment where missing evidence is not just inconvenient but risky.

Over time the scope became wider and more deliberate. The platform moved from a transactional learning system into a workforce platform supporting appraisals, recognition, competency work, reporting, and adjacent operational processes where structured data and automation reduce manual effort.

The portfolio is not just a delivery story. It is a record of product direction, decision-making under constraint, discovery in progress, and the effort to move an inherited programme into a more product-led operating model.

Product Vision
The product case in one page
Our LMS will be measured by how well it helps staff and managers complete, evidence and report workforce learning, appraisal and competency requirements efficiently — while reducing duplication, improving data quality, and removing avoidable administrative effort across people processes. Where appropriate, the platform will also be used to streamline related organisational workflows where structured data, automation and visibility can reduce manual effort and improve consistency.

Current Product Surface
Digital Appraisals
Live
Fully digital appraisal process replacing paper-based system. 12-month controlled pilot followed by full organisational rollout. Iterated across multiple versions based on real staff feedback.
13,000+ staff · 20–30 mins saved per appraisal · Half the time vs paper
Staff Awards Nominations
Live
LMS-based nomination system replacing a manual free-text process. Active Directory integration enables verified staff and team selection. Significant reduction in administrative processing time.
Replaces manual daily spreadsheet process · 2,300+ nominations per cycle
Nursing Competency Framework
Pilot
Consolidating fragmented nursing competency requirements into one trackable platform. Starting with a single competency — users involved from discovery stage. Deliberate sequencing based on lessons from appraisal rollout.
Iteration one user testing complete · Feedback captured · Iteration two planned with users
Manager Reporting & Visibility
In Progress
Enabling line managers to view and report on their team's compliance status directly from the platform. Exploring ad hoc group configuration as an interim solution while deeper integration is developed.
Critical path to North Star metric · Interim solution being designed
Strategic Bets
Strategic Bets — Now

Manager Visibility — give managers direct access to team readiness data. Workforce Automation — eliminate manual enrolment and ESR upload processes. Complete nursing framework iteration two with users. Resolve manager hierarchy data and DPIA dependencies.

Strategic Bets — Next

Career Development — AHP framework, ACP portfolio, nursing expansion. Workforce Experience — new starter pathway, monthly recognition, volunteer access. Generate Workforce Insight — skills heat maps, pillar-level reporting, learning demand forecasting.
