Outcomes
KPI dashboard · What actually changed
👥 Platform Scale
20K
Staff now have access to a learning platform that works. Given what the legacy system looked like on arrival, this is not a low bar.
📋 Appraisal Transformation
13K+
Staff moved from paper to fully digital appraisals following a 12-month controlled pilot. Staff described it as easier and faster than the paper process.
🚀 Roadshow Impact
95%+
Increase in appraisal completions among participating staff over the two-week roadshow period. System data — not a survey or estimate.
🛡 Governance by Design
Legacy failures not replicated
Restricted permission architecture held from day one. The content governance failures of the legacy system have not recurred. Outcomes measured in problems that haven't happened count.
